How this board works

One shared list of opportunities, from first hunch to committed pursuit.

This is the pipeline for funding calls, residencies, grants, and partnerships INLAND might pursue. Anyone on the team can suggest something. Claude (the Scout) researches each suggestion in a working session. The team then decides what to pursue and what to set aside. Every change here is saved straight to the project's GitHub repository, so the board is the single source of truth.

The team
Two states

Why some cards are locked

An opportunity is one of two things, and the board treats them differently:

Awaiting research

Suggested, not yet scanned

Added by a person but not researched yet. It can’t move to Pursuing until Claude has scanned it. You can still archive it if it’s clearly not a fit. These sit at the bottom of the Inbox column.

Researched

Scanned and ready

Claude has filled in the fit score, rationale, budget, and documents. It carries a FIT badge and can move freely to Pursuing or Archived. These sit at the top of the Inbox column.

The columns

What goes where

Inbox
The intake column, split in two: Ready to pursue (researched) on top, Awaiting research (locked) below.
Pursuing
Opportunities the team has committed to. Each shows a production badge — Awaiting production until Claude has drafted the documents, then Docs drafted with links. Give each an owner.
Archived
Closed, expired, or declined. Out of the way but never deleted by accident.
Moving cards

Buttons or drag — your choice

Use the “→” buttons on a card, or grab the handle and drag it to another column. On a phone, hold the handle briefly, then drag. The lock still applies: an awaiting card won’t drop into Pursuing until it’s been researched.

Reading a card

Card anatomy

Cards are collapsed by default so the board stays scannable. Tap a card to expand it.

Fit 5 → 1
How well it suits INLAND: 5 bullseye, 4 strong, 3 possible, 2 weak, 1 marginal. Set during research.
Deadline
Shows days remaining; turns oxblood and reads “urgent” inside 21 days.
Owner
Who’s carrying it. Edit it, then press Save on the card to commit.
Notes
Shared scratchpad for context, contacts, and next steps. Your edits stage on the card until you Save.
Open call ↗
Link to the original page describing the opportunity.
Flag
Mark an opportunity as priority. Flagged cards rise to the top of their column and Claude produces them first when several are in Pursuing.
Documents
Links to what Claude has created — the research brief, and later the full application set. Files live in the project's GitHub repo, so anyone with access can open them.
The buttons

What each control does

+ Suggest
Opens the form to add a new opportunity (name, link, your name).
→ Pursuing / Inbox / Archived
Moves a card between columns. Applied immediately. Hidden or locked when a move isn’t allowed.
Save / Discard
Appear on a card once you edit its Owner or Notes. Save commits just that card’s changes; Discard reverts them. Moves, flags, and deletes apply immediately and need no Save.
All changes saved
The indicator in the header: reads Saving… while committing, Unsaved · N when cards have pending edits, and All changes saved when everything’s committed.
Delete
Removes a card permanently, after a confirmation. Use Archive instead when in doubt.
You / Set your name
Sets the name tagged to your edits and suggestions.
Refresh
Re-pulls the latest board in case someone else made a change.
Sign out
Ends your session and returns to the password screen.
