# Timeline — INLAND Angola
### Goethe-Institut · Africa–Europe Partnerships for Culture

*Indicative; align exact dates to the live programme windows in GAP (mobility deadlines are
rolling quarterly; the Visual Arts round has its own annual deadline). Mobilities/projects must
begin on/after the programme's stated start dates. Two seasonal anchors fix the schedule: the
**coffee harvest** (≈May–August) and the **Festa da Ilha / Kianda** (second Friday of November).*

| Phase | Window (indicative) | Activity |
|---|---|---|
| 0 · Set-up | On approval | Sign partner confirmation (Lueji A'Nkonde University / Luanda co-partner); confirm INLAND legal entity; inform Goethe/programme contact |
| 1 · Scouting (Mobility) | Next eligible mobility window | EU photographer + partner meet custodians across Lunda, Huambo, Huíla and the Ilha de Luanda; confirm consent; **identify named sona masters, a coffee family and a potter**; lock the 3-cluster plan |
| 2 · Pre-production | ~4 weeks after scouting | Final story list, shoot schedule, contributor contracts, logistics with partner |
| 3 · Cluster A — Lunda (East) | Dry-season camps | *Sona* masters (Saurimo/Dundo) · *mukanda* / *mwana pwo* mask rite |
| 4 · Cluster B — Planalto | Coffee harvest (≈May–Aug) | *Ondjango* council fire (Bailundo) · Amboim robusta revival (Kwanza Sul) · potters/weavers |
| 5 · Cluster C — South & Coast | Dry-season transhumance | Mwila/Mucubal herds (Huíla/Namibe) · *efundula* (Cunene, consent-led) · salt & fishing (Baía Farta) |
| 6 · Signature + memory strand | Around the November feast | Festa da Ilha / **Kianda** (Ilha de Luanda); *lumbu* of Mbanza Kongo; Njinga's footprints (Pungo Andongo) |
| 7 · Edit & design | ~8–10 weeks | Photo edit, writing, film cut, edition layout (INLAND house style) |
| 8 · Presentation — Angola | Within project window | Exhibition + community presentations with the partner; custodians receive prints/credit |
| 9 · Presentation — Europe | Within project window | Exhibition leg in Europe; edition published (≈170–240 pp) |
| 10 · Reporting | Per programme | Financial report and proof of expenditure to Goethe |

**Critical-path dependencies:** signed partner confirmation → mobility scouting →
consent-locked story list → production. The *efundula* and *mukanda* stories proceed **only**
with community consent secured during scouting; the Kianda signature scene is fixed to the
**November** feast.
