## Fit — 2/5 (weak — surfaced because the week is thin)
Low-burden (submit an existing edit) and real visibility (Vogue publication + festival), **but a thematic stretch**. The 2026 brief — *"Creativity as Rebellion," a position in relation to the world as it is* — rewards a contemporary, statement-driven, oppositional register that runs against INLAND's discipline of **continuity over crisis, dignity over spectacle, tension not attack**. An INLAND series could be framed as quiet resistance-through-persistence (traditions surviving standardization), but that reframing is a reach. Include only as an opportunistic visibility entry, not a priority.
