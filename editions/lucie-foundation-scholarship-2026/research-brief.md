# Research brief — Lucie Foundation Scholarship 2026 (Documentary / Photojournalism)

- **Funder / host:** Lucie Foundation (House of Lucie)
- **Channel:** Public fund (photography scholarship/grant)
- **Link:** https://www.luciefoundation.org/programs/scholarship/
- **Deadline:** Summer 2026 — *to confirm* (call is live now; finalists & winners announced October 2026)
- **Budget / award:** Cash prizes — US$3,000 (Professional) / US$1,000 (Emerging) + camera gear; winners join a group exhibition that tours House of Lucie galleries (Los Angeles, Athens, Budapest, **Ostuni (Italy)**, Samui)
- **Fit score:** 3 / 5

## Why it fits INLAND
The scholarship runs a dedicated **Documentary / Photojournalism** category, open worldwide to professional and emerging photographers (18+). The documentary register is a genuine fit for INLAND's photographic practice. The award itself is modest (a prize, not edition funding), but the **touring exhibition — including Ostuni in Puglia** — is a useful visibility and relationship foothold in Italy, and a low-burden entry (20-image edit + proposal).

## Eligibility note
Applicant is an individual photographer; INLAND's route is via a commissioned photographer entering an edition's documentary body of work.

## Candidate territory / angle
Open — any INLAND edition's documentary body of work. Best entered as a cohesive, already-shot project on a specific territory's custodians.

## Required application documents
- 20-image project edit (existing project being continued, or a prior cohesive project)
- Project proposal / statement
- Photographer bio
- Submission fee (~US$15 emerging / US$25 professional per entry)
