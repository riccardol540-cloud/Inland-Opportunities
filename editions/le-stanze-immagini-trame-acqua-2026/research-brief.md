# Research brief — Le Stanze della Fotografia, Open Call 2026 "Immagini e trame d'acqua"

- **Title:** Open Call 2026 — *Immagini e trame d'acqua* ("Images and Weaves of Water")
- **Funder / host:** Fondazione Le Stanze della Fotografia (Venice) with the Associazione per il Patrimonio delle Colline del Prosecco di Conegliano e Valdobbiadene
- **Channel:** Public fund / foundation photography programme
- **Link:** https://www.lestanzedellafotografia.it/en/homepage/open-call
- **Deadline:** 30 September 2026 (unless extended)
- **Award / what you get:** Selection into a curated exhibition at Le Stanze della Fotografia — a major photography venue on the Isola di San Giorgio Maggiore, Venice. Free entry; no cash grant confirmed. Value is visibility, curatorial validation, and a Venice relationship foothold.
- **Eligibility:** Free and open internationally — citizens, students, young authors, photographers, videomakers, writers, and collectives; adults only. INLAND (Italy-based, published photographers/writers) qualifies directly.

## Theme

Testimonies, stories and impressions of **water within cultural and landscape heritage**. The
call starts from the Prosecco Hills of Conegliano and Valdobbiadene (a UNESCO World Heritage
site) but explicitly invites work relating to **other UNESCO heritage sites in Italy and
worldwide**. Three submission categories: **photography, writing, video** — narratives that
unite the visual dimension with personal reflection. Submit by email to opencallstanze@gmail.com.

## Fit for INLAND — score 3/5

Adjacent-to-strong. It is not edition-scale funding (an exhibition selection, not a grant), but
it is squarely in INLAND's register — place-based visual storytelling, heritage, and the "before
it changes" gaze — and the multi-format (photo + text + video) structure mirrors INLAND's own
output. Best treated as a **low-burden visibility and relationship foothold** with a prestigious
Venetian institution, and as a way to field-test a water-heritage lead ahead of a fuller edition.

## Candidate territory / INLAND angle

**Water as social infrastructure and ritual memory** — the INLAND "human–nature relationship
under ecological pressure" theme. Rather than a landscape-postcard of water, anchor to named
custodians and a seasonal scene in an under-documented Italian water culture, e.g.:

- **Sardinia — the lagoon (stagni) fishers of Cabras / Santa Giusta**: the last builders and
  rowers of *fassoni* reed boats, the *bottarga* (grey-mullet roe) economy, and the barefoot
  running of the simulacrum to San Salvatore each late summer — a locatable place, named
  custodians, and a real seasonal ritual.
- **Po Delta — Comacchio valli**: eel-fishing *casoni*, the *vallanti* and the marsh-water
  calendar of a shrinking lagoon economy.

Either reads as INLAND: continuity over crisis, dignity over spectacle, water as the thing that
organises a community's year.
