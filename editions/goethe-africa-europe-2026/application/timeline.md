# Timeline — INLAND Rwanda
### Goethe-Institut · Africa–Europe Partnerships for Culture

*Indicative; align exact dates to the live programme windows in GAP (mobility deadlines are
rolling quarterly; the Visual Arts round has its own annual deadline). Mobilities/projects
must begin on/after the programme's stated start dates.*

| Phase | Window (indicative) | Activity |
|---|---|---|
| 0 · Set-up | On approval | Sign partner confirmation; confirm INLAND legal entity; inform Goethe/programme contact |
| 1 · Scouting (Mobility) | Next eligible mobility window | EU photographer + partner meet custodians across Musanze, Rutsiro, Huye; confirm consent and lock the 3-track plan; **field-verify Kubandwa** |
| 2 · Pre-production | ~4 weeks after scouting | Final story list, shoot schedule, contributor contracts, logistics with partner |
| 3 · Track A — Musanze | Dry-season field period | Batwa knowledge · uburozi healers · ikigage (1 base) |
| 4 · Track B — Brewing & Land | Toward a wedding/harvest | urwagwa (Rutsiro) · Gishwati (Nyabihu) · kivuguto |
| 5 · Track C — Coffee & Memory | Coffee harvest; August for Umuganura | Cooperatives · Umuganura (Huro) · imigongo |
| 6 · Edit & design | ~8–10 weeks | Photo edit, writing, film cut, edition layout (INLAND house style) |
| 7 · Presentation — Rwanda | Within project window | Exhibition + community presentations with the partner; custodians receive prints/credit |
| 8 · Presentation — Europe | Within project window | Exhibition leg in Europe; edition published (≈170–240 pp) |
| 9 · Reporting | Per programme | Financial report and proof of expenditure to Goethe |

**Critical-path dependencies:** signed partner confirmation → mobility scouting →
consent-locked story list → production. The Kubandwa story proceeds **only** if confirmed
and consented during scouting.
