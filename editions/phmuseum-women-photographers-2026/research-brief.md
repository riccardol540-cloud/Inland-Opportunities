# Research brief — PhMuseum 2026 Women Photographers Grant

- **Title**: PhMuseum 2026 Women Photographers Grant
- **Funder / host**: PhMuseum (Bologna, Italy)
- **Channel**: public-fund (independent photography prize/grant)
- **Link**: https://phmuseum.com/grants/2026-women-photographers-grant
- **Deadline**: **~10 October 2026** (final entry; reduced fee €25 until 19 Sept, €30 until 10 Oct). Judging Oct–Nov; shortlist before 20 Nov 2026. **Live, not urgent.**
- **Budget / award size**: **€10,000** total cash prize pool across categories, plus publication, exhibition and portfolio visibility on PhMuseum.
- **Eligibility**: Open **worldwide to women and non-binary photographers, 18+**, professional or emerging; single images and series accepted. INLAND applies **via a commissioned woman photographer** presenting an edition's body of work (the same applicant model as the Smith / Lucie entries already on the board).

Modest money, real visibility. A solid opportunistic 3, best paired with an edition already in production.
