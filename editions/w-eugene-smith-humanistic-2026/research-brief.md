# Research brief — W. Eugene Smith Grant in Humanistic Photography 2026

- **Funder / host:** W. Eugene Smith Memorial Fund
- **Channel:** Public fund (documentary photography grant)
- **Link:** https://www.smithfund.org/eugene-smith-grant
- **Deadline:** ~late September 2026 (recurring annual window — call opens ~1 July, closes late Sept / early Oct). *To confirm on smithfund.org when the 2026 cycle posts.*
- **Budget / award:** US$30,000 main grant + two US$10,000 finalist grants
- **Fit score:** 4 / 5

## Why it fits INLAND
The Smith Grant honours a photographer "whose past work and proposed project follows the tradition of W. Eugene Smith's concerned photography and dedicated compassion" — long-form, humanistic, essayistic documentary about the human condition (social, cultural, environmental significance). That is precisely INLAND's register: slow, intimate, anthropological storytelling grounded in named custodians and real places. A meaningful, production-scale award that could underwrite a photographer's extended field embedding for an edition.

## Eligibility note
Open to photographers of any nationality; the applicant is an individual photographer, not a publication. INLAND's route is to apply **with / through a commissioned photographer** on an edition's body of work (e.g. the photographer documenting custodians for an in-progress edition), or to nominate that photographer. In scope per the Scout rubric ("photography/documentary grants … when they fit the editorial profile").

## Candidate territory / angle
Open — pairs with any in-progress INLAND edition. The strongest angle is to enter the photographer's accumulating body of work on a specific edition's custodians (a named protagonist, a locatable place, a seasonal scene), framed as concerned-but-dignified documentary in the Smith tradition.

## Required application documents
- Project proposal / statement (proposed continuation)
- Portfolio of past work (a prior cohesive project)
- Proposed-project image edit
- Photographer bio / CV
- Application fee (fee waivers available)
