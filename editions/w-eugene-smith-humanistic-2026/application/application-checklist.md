# Application — Checklist, fee & submission
### W. Eugene Smith Grant in Humanistic Photography 2026

> Operational checklist for submitting. **Figures below are from the 2025 cycle and must be
> re-confirmed on smithfund.org once the 2026 call posts (it typically opens ~1 July).**

---

## The grant
- **Award:** US $30,000 main grant + two US $10,000 finalist grants.
- **Eligibility:** photographers of any nationality, 18+; honours long-form humanistic
  documentary in the tradition of W. Eugene Smith. **Applicant is an individual
  photographer** — INLAND applies *with/through* its commissioned Georgia photographer.
- **Cycle:** call opens ~1 July; deadline **late September / early October** (2025 deadline
  was 7 Oct). **Confirm the 2026 deadline.**

## Fee & waiver
- **Entry fee:** US $50 (2025). Pay online at submission.
- **Fee waivers:** a limited number are offered via partner organisations (e.g. Magnum
  Foundation, Women Photograph, Diversify Photo, the Everyday Projects, Foto Féminas, Black
  Women Photographers, Indigenous Photograph, Market Photo Workshop, and others). Waivers
  must be requested **from a partner org ~2 weeks before the deadline** (2025: by 23 Sep).
  **If a waiver is wanted, start this early.**

## Required materials (2025 format — re-confirm 2026)
- [ ] **Project description** — ≤ 2,000 characters → `project-statement.md`
- [ ] **Project proposal** — ≤ 3,000 characters → `project-statement.md`
- [ ] **Biography** — ≤ 1,500 characters → `photographer-bio-cv.md`
- [ ] **CV** — ≤ 2,000 characters, or a PDF of any length → `photographer-bio-cv.md`
- [ ] **Images** — up to **40** total (past work + proposed project) →
      `portfolio-past-work.md` + `proposed-project-image-edit.md`
- [ ] **Entry fee** US $50 (or approved waiver)

## Submission
- **Where:** online via the Smith Fund submission portal (linked from
  smithfund.org/eugene-smith-grant).
- **Image specs:** confirm the portal's file format, dimensions and max file size for the
  2026 cycle before exporting the edit.

## Pre-submission gaps to close *(human input)*
1. **Name the applicant photographer** and confirm authorship of the Georgia portfolio.
2. **Confirm 2026 dates, fee, image count/specs** on smithfund.org once the call posts.
3. **Decide on a fee waiver** and, if yes, request it from a partner org early.
4. **Re-contact and obtain consent** from named protagonists appearing in the edit
   (shepherd family, Chelidze family, Riho, Turkia family, khevisberi/khatis diasakhlisi,
   Duisi zikr circle, Kakhetian winemaker, Oni/Kutaisi communities, David Gareja).
5. **Verify field windows** — transhumance, Atengenoba, rtveli, David Gareja border access
   (see `timeline.md`).
