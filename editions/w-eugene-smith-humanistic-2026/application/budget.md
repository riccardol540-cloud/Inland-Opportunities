# Application — Budget (indicative)
### W. Eugene Smith Grant 2026 · "The Quiet Country"

> **Note:** the Smith Grant is a fixed award (US $30,000 main grant) and does **not** require
> a line-item budget in the application. This is an **internal planning budget** showing how
> the award would underwrite the extended field embedding the project needs. Figures are
> indicative and to be confirmed with INLAND production. (USD; field rates are Georgia-low.)

## How the US $30,000 would be used

| Line | Detail | Indicative (USD) |
|---|---|---|
| Field embedding — Cluster A (Eastern Highlands) | Tusheti transhumance (late May + Sept return), guda dairy, Khevsureti / Atengenoba (mid-Jul), Pankisi (Fridays). ~5–6 weeks across the season. | 7,500 |
| Field embedding — Cluster B (Western Voices) | Svaneti (towers, Riho, Svan youth), Guria (Lanchkhuti), Oni/Kutaisi. ~4 weeks. | 6,000 |
| Field embedding — Cluster C (Plain & Desert) | Kakheti rtveli (late Sept), Tbilisi minankari, David Gareja. ~3 weeks. | 4,000 |
| In-country transport | 4x4 hire and fuel for high passes (Abano, Datvisjvari), driver where needed, internal travel. | 3,500 |
| Local fixers / translation | Svan, Kist (Chechen), Khevsur access; community introductions; consent liaison. | 3,000 |
| Accommodation & subsistence | Guesthouses and shepherd-camp stays across ~12–13 field weeks. | 3,000 |
| Equipment & consumables | Cold/altitude gear, backup bodies/cards, hard drives, insurance. | 1,500 |
| Post-production | Editing, scanning/processing, the 40-frame edit, captioning with custodians. | 1,500 |
| **Total** | | **30,000** |

## Notes
- The grant funds the **photographer's field work**; INLAND covers editorial overhead and
  the printed edition (~170–240 pp) through its existing funding model (institutional
  partners and print sales at €30/issue).
- Seasonality drives cost: three trips timed to **late May, mid-July (Atengenoba) and late
  September (rtveli + autumn descent)** rather than one continuous stay.
- Consent and fixer lines are deliberately funded — access to the shrine cult, the zikr and
  the synagogues depends on trusted local introductions, not transactions.
- All figures **to confirm** with INLAND production before submission.
