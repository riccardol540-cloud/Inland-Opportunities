# Research brief — Albania (user suggestion, enriched by Scout)

## Why Albania is a genuine INLAND territory
Albania fits the blueprint cleanly: a loud international story (isolation under Hoxha, the
bunkers, post-1991 emigration) that **crowds out a much quieter country** of living
traditions still visible and photographable today. Rich, under-documented, endangered
knowledge systems held by **named custodians in locatable places, with real seasonal
scenes** — the three-part quality bar is easy to meet here.

## Candidate story threads (desk-level; each needs field verification & consent)
- **Albanian folk iso-polyphony** (UNESCO ICH) — Lab/Tosk drone-and-countermelody singing
  around **Gjirokastër, Përmet, Vlorë**; sung at weddings, funerals and feasts. Endangered
  by rural exodus and lack of support for practitioners; Gjirokastër recently opened a
  **Museum of Iso-Polyphony inside Cold-War bunkers**. Scene: a *sofra*/feast rehearsal or
  a village festival season. *(Named ensembles/masters to be identified on the ground.)*
- **Highland Kanun world & the *burrnesha* ("sworn virgins")** of the northern Alps
  (Dukagjin, Theth, Kelmend) — women who took a vow to live as men; a dwindling handful
  remain. Locatable villages, named individuals in prior reporting (must be re-contacted
  and must consent before any public pitch).
- **Vjosa river communities** — the Vjosa became Europe's first **Wild River National Park
  (2023)**; pastoral and riverine livelihoods along one of the continent's last free-flowing
  rivers, under change. Human–nature-under-pressure thread with a clear season.
- **Aromanian / Vlach pastoralism** — transhumant shepherd communities (e.g. around
  Voskopojë/Korçë), an endangered language and a seasonal migration = strong visual scene.
- **Lake Ohrid / Lin** and lagoon fishing cultures — food-as-memory and water-heritage angle.

## Realistic funding routes (no single call locked yet)
- **British Council — Connections Through Culture 2026** (on the board, URGENT 12 Aug 2026):
  UK↔Italy seed grant; could fund a UK partner leg but is **not Albania-specific**.
- **Creative Europe — European Cooperation Projects** (next annual round; 2026 round closed
  5 May): the natural home for a translocal Italy–Albania edition with partners.
- **MiC / DGCC "Strategia Fotografia"** (on the board) via the **Arbëreshë** (Albanian-
  speaking communities of southern Italy) — an Italy-anchored bridge to the Albania subject.
- **Allianz Kulturstiftung "Bringing Europe Together"** (on the board) — its lead candidate
  is already the **Arbëreshë ↔ Albania** dialogue; the closest existing fundable frame.
- **UNESCO CC4WBs (Culture & Creativity for the Western Balkans)** — Albania is an eligible
  IPA beneficiary, but the applicant must be **resident in the Western Balkans**, so INLAND
  can only reach it **through an Albanian resident partner** (and the 2026 small-grants call
  closed 30 Jun 2026 — watch for the next round).
