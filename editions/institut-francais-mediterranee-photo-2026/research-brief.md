# Research brief — Institut français · Mediterranean photography support (Publication + Production)

- **Title**: Aide à la publication / production d'œuvres photographiques — "monde méditerranéen"
- **Funder / host**: Institut français (French cultural institute network)
- **Channel**: cultural-institute
- **Link**: https://www.institutfrancais.com/en/programs
- **Deadline**: **2026-10-01** (both strands)
- **Budget / award size**: Publication strand — editing & printing/production costs of the photo book **covered in full**. Production strand — production support for new photographic work on a Mediterranean theme (amount per project).
- **Eligibility (why INLAND qualifies)**: The publication aid is open to **all professional photographers who have already exhibited or published their images, regardless of age or nationality** — so an Italy-based publisher/photographer team qualifies. The only hard condition is thematic: the project must take the **Mediterranean world** as its subject. Publication strand requires a **completed** photographic + editorial project (i.e. it funds bringing a finished body of work to print).
- **Fit score**: **4 — Strong**
- **Rationale**: Nationality-open, theme-locked to the Mediterranean, and it covers exactly INLAND's cost centres — a finished photographic edition's editing and printing (publication) or a fresh field shoot (production). INLAND Issue 03 (*Isole Minori*) already proves the Mediterranean register; this funds the next one.

## Possible territory / angle

The grant **sets the geography** (the Mediterranean world) and leaves the country open, so pick the Mediterranean territory with the richest under-documented living traditions and a feasible partner. Candidate angles (run the INLAND quality bar — named custodian, locatable place, seasonal scene — on the one chosen):

- **Northern Morocco — the Rif & Tétouan**: Jbala mountain culture, women weavers, Andalusian musical heritage, mountain agrarian life. (Also the territory of the Anna Lindh Matera/Tétouan call on the board — a possible two-grant stack.)
- **Tunisian islands & coast**: Kerkennah fixed-fishing *charfia*, Djerba's Ibadi and Jewish continuities, oasis agriculture.
- **Southern Italy / Basilicata**: building outward from *Isole Minori* into the mainland's transhumance, ritual and grain cultures (Matera tie-in).

Frame per `research-angle.md`: the loud Mediterranean story (migration, borders) stays as background; INLAND goes to the quieter country — what persists in named families, villages and seasonal practices.

## Two strands, two pipeline stages

- **Production support** → fund the field shoot for a Mediterranean edition that is not yet photographed.
- **Publication aid** → fund the editing and printing of a Mediterranean body of work that is already complete.

Producer should choose the strand that matches how far the chosen edition has progressed.

## Required application documents (for the Producer)

- Completed (or, for production, planned) photographic project — image edit / portfolio
- Editorial / publication project description (the book), Mediterranean-theme statement
- Photographer track record (prior exhibition or publication)
- Budget (editing & production costs)
- Timeline to publication
