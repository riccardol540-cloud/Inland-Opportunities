# Timeline to publication — INLAND Tunisia
### Institut français · Mediterranean photography support

*Planning timeline for review, anchored to the call **deadline of 1 October 2026** and to
Tunisia's season-locked scenes. Dates are indicative **[confirm against the IF project window
and the 2026/27 festival and fishing calendars]**. The shoot is built around three seasonal
windows — **charfia (autumn)**, **matanza & orange blossom (spring)**, **cork & malouf
(summer)** — which set the order of the clusters.*

---

| Phase | Window | Work |
|---|---|---|
| **Pre-production** | done → Sep 2026 | Desk research complete (this corpus); lock named custodians with contacts in `../INLAND_Tunisia_Topics_Contacts.xlsx`; confirm IF strand(s), routing and the Institut français de Tunisie partnership. |
| **Submission** | by **1 Oct 2026** | Submit to the Institut français Mediterranean photography support (production and/or publication). |
| **Award & prep** | late 2026 | On award: contracts, consents, fixer/interpreter engagement, insurance, scheduling around seasons. |
| **Shoot — The South & the Islands** | **autumn 2026 / 2027** | The **charfia** net-setting (Kerkennah, autumn) ✦; Djerba (with the El Ghriba pilgrimage shot in **spring**); the Dahar ksour; the Gabès oasis. |
| **Shoot — Cap Bon & the Sahel** | **spring 2027** | The Sidi Daoud **matanza** (May–July) and Nabeul **orange blossom** (Apr–May); Mahdia silk (around a wedding); Kairouan carpets & makroudh. |
| **Shoot — The Green North** | **summer 2027** | Sejnane pottery; Testour **malouf** festival; Stambeli nights in Tunis (year-round, consent-led); the Kroumirie **cork** stripping. |
| **Edit & design** | autumn 2027 | Photo edit and sequencing (12 essays, 3 clusters); long-form text; design in INLAND house style; short film cut. |

| **Publication & exhibition** | **2028** | Printed INLAND Tunisia edition released; touring exhibition proposed in Tunisia (IF de Tunisie) and Europe; community presentations returning the work to custodians. |

---

## Critical-path notes
- **Seasons drive the order, not convenience:** the charfia autumn-set, the El Ghriba spring
  pilgrimage, the matanza and orange blossom (spring), and the cork stripping (summer) are
  fixed windows; missing one slips that story a full year.
- **El Ghriba (spring) and the Testour malouf festival** dates must be confirmed early and the
  shoot booked around them.
- **Stambeli** ceremonies are arranged with the house on the community's own calendar and
  consent — not scheduled for the camera.
- If IF runs production and publication as **separate calls**, the publication submission moves
  to **2027** once the shoot is complete, with print in winter 2027/28.
