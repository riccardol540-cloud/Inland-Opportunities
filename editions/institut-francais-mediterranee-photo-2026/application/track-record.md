## INLAND in brief

INLAND is a yearly print publication, founded **2021**, documenting the soul of a country
before it becomes history, through named, locatable custodians of living traditions. Each issue
is a year-long, place-based immersion in a single territory, published as a printed magazine of
~170–240 pages (retail €30) plus digital stories, photography and film. Funded through cultural
institutions and partnerships, not advertising. Italy / EU-based.

Contact: info@inland-mag.com · @inland.mag · inland-mag.com
