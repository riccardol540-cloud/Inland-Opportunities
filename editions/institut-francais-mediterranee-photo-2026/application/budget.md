*Planning budget for review. **All figures are planning assumptions** to be confirmed against
the live IF finance template and real quotes. Currency: EUR. Two parts matching the two

season-locked (charfia autumn; matanza & blossom spring; cork summer), so the shoot runs in

## Notes & assumptions to confirm
- **Strand structure:** confirm on the IF portal whether publication and production are one
  call or two, and the **ceiling** for each (not published in the open call) — adjust the
  request lines accordingly.
- **Applicant & routing:** confirm whether the applicant must be the photographer or the
  publisher, and whether disbursement routes via a **French organisation** (a French
  co-applicant may be required); the Institut français de Tunisie may be the routing partner.
- **Publication aid covers editing & printing "in full":** if so, move the full Part B onto the
  IF line and reduce the co-financing share against printing.
- **Print run & paper** to be re-quoted with INLAND's printer; €12,000 is a placeholder for a
  ~200 pp run of 1,500–2,000 copies.
- **Custodian honoraria** to be agreed with the people, not set unilaterally; **season-locked**
  shoots (charfia autumn, matanza/blossom spring, cork summer) drive the trip structure and
  travel cost.
